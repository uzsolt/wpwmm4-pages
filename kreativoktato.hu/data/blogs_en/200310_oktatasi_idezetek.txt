We love quotes and usually resort to reading them for inspiration.
<p>Do you sometimes lose sight of why you teach and what your aims
are? Say no more, we understand. Let’s take a look at these quotes
related to education. We love quotes and usually resort to reading
them for inspiration. We have to admit, it works. We hope they will
also help you.</p>
